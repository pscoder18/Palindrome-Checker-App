### Use Case 6 (UC6): Queue + Stack Fairness Check
* **Goal:** Demonstrate palindrome validation by combining and comparing two different data structures: Queue and Stack.
* **Key Concepts:** * **Queue (FIFO):** First-In-First-Out behavior. Characters are removed in the exact order they were inserted (reading left-to-right).
    * **Stack (LIFO):** Last-In-First-Out behavior. Characters are removed in the reverse order of insertion (reading right-to-left).
    * **Symmetric Comparison:** By removing one element from the front of the queue and one from the top of the stack simultaneously, the program naturally compares the word from both ends.
    * **Interfaces vs. Classes:** Using `LinkedList` to instantiate the `Queue` interface.
* **Data Structures:** `Queue` & `Stack`
